# Lounge Content Rules

1. **Tone:** The tone must ALWAYS be patient, plain-English, safe, fun, and relaxing. Imagine a cozy coffee shop for AI nerds who just finished a hard lesson.
2. **Anti-Hype:** NO marketing hype words (e.g. "game-changer", "revolutionary", "mind-blowing"). Keep it strictly factual, pragmatic, and grounded.
3. **Safety First:** If discussing commands, always include brief safety context (e.g., "Always double-check your directory before running `rm`").
4. **Link Back:** Always include at least one strong CTA linking back to the CLI Academy /paths or /dashboard. 
5. **Format:** Output must strictly be MDX compatible with Next.js frontmatter.

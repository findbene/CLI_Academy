# Launch Plan

Project: CLI Academy
Version: 1.0

## 1. Launch Philosophy

We launch the product in stages.
The goal is not one big moment.
The goal is compounding momentum, trust, and paid validation.

## 2. Launch Phases

### Phase A - Internal readiness

- repo and app scaffold complete
- first free path high quality
- tutor working
- basic analytics working
- core learner flow tested

### Phase B - Closed alpha

- 10 to 20 invited users
- observe onboarding friction
- observe first-win rate
- collect lesson confusion points

### Phase C - Small beta

- 25 to 75 users
- free path live
- paid paths still gated until content fully ready
- launch content starts publishing

### Phase D - Soft public launch

- free path open publicly
- YouTube and X distribution begins
- email capture active
- upgrade waitlist or early paid access

### Phase E - Monetized rollout

- paid content real and complete
- annual plan available
- downloads polished
- support and trust surfaces in place

## 3. Messaging Priorities

Lead with:

- "get it working"
- "do it safely"
- "learn it fast"
- "real outcomes, not fluff"

Do not lead with:

- "our AI is smarter"
- abstract enterprise transformation claims
- hype-heavy model talk

## 4. Channel Mix

### Owned

- website
- email list
- product itself

### Rented

- YouTube
- X
- LinkedIn
- Reddit

### Borrowed

- creator collaborations
- guest content
- partner mentions

## 5. Launch Assets Needed

- landing page
- pricing page
- free path page
- waitlist or signup flow
- first 5 to 10 short videos
- core PDF download
- launch email sequence
- known issues page
- trust and freshness copy

## 6. What Success Looks Like

### Activation

- users complete onboarding
- users start the first lesson
- users complete the first lesson
- users get value from the tutor

### Monetization

- free users upgrade after real value moments
- annual plan gets meaningful adoption
- first paid users are satisfied enough to stay

### Trust

- no misleading pricing copy
- no unsafe docs
- no obviously stale content

## 7. First Launch Metrics

- onboarding completion rate
- first lesson completion rate
- tutor usage rate
- lab verification success rate
- free to pro conversion

## 8. Launch Rule

Do not launch paid access until:

- paid paths are real
- assets are real
- tutor policy is consistent
- upgrade copy is honest

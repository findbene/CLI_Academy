# Claude CoWork Session Brief Template

Use this template when you want CoWork to produce something substantial without wandering.

```markdown
# Task Brief

## Goal

What do you want Claude CoWork to produce?

## Audience

Who is this for?

## Deliverable

What should the final output look like?
- format:
- length:
- tone:

## Inputs

What files, notes, or source material should CoWork use?

## Constraints

What should CoWork avoid?
- avoid factual guessing
- flag uncertainty clearly
- keep sensitive material out of the output

## What Good Looks Like

List 3-5 qualities of a strong result.

## Review Check

Before you finish, confirm:
- the output matches the audience
- the output uses the supplied material
- assumptions are called out
- sections are organized clearly
```

## Tip

If CoWork gives generic output, your brief is probably too vague. Tighten the goal, audience, and deliverable before retrying.

# Lock in the learning loop

## Objective

Before you move on to the more advanced paths (like installing Claude Code or deploying servers), you need to internalize the CLI Academy Learning Loop. 

This platform does not work if you passively read it. It is an active engine.

## Walkthrough

For every future lesson on this platform, you will follow this 4-step loop:

### 1. Try
Read the walkthrough and try the commands on your own machine. Do not skip steps.

### 2. Get Stuck
You **will** get stuck. You will see red error text. This is a normal part of engineering. When it happens, do not close the window.

### 3. Ask Tutor
Open the Floating Tutor (bottom right). Change your learning mode to **Hint-based** on the left. Explain your error to the Tutor and let it guide you toward the answer instead of doing it for you.

### 4. Verify
At the bottom of every lesson is a **Verification Block**. Paste your final output or code into it. The platform will automatically verify your success. Do not move to the next lesson until you pass the verification block!

# Create the CLI Academy workspace

## Objective

We are going to start by building a digital safety net. When you're learning AI commands, you never want your agent accidentally reading or modifying personal files on your Desktop or Documents folder. 

Today we are going to create a brand new, empty, and perfectly safe folder on your computer called `cli-academy`. Everything you do in this platform will happen inside that folder.

## Walkthrough

Let's keep this simple.
1. Open your computer's file explorer (Finder on Mac, File Explorer on Windows).
2. Go to your main Drive (like your `C:` drive or your home user folder).
3. Right-click, select **New Folder**, and name it exactly: `cli-academy`.
4. Open that new folder.
5. Create three more folders inside it:
   - `notes` (Where we'll keep copies of configurations)
   - `projects` (Where we'll let Claude write code)
   - `screenshots` (Where we'll capture errors)

# Set up your first bounded Cowork task

## Objective

Our goal in this lesson is to empower you to create your very first *bounded* Cowork task brief. Think of "bounded" as putting a helpful fence around your task, clearly showing Cowork exactly what it needs to do, what information it has, and what a successful outcome looks like. This isn't just good practice; it's the safest way to work with AI, ensuring Cowork understands your intent and delivers results you expect, minimizing surprises. By the end, you'll have a clear task brief ready for Cowork to tackle!

## Walkthrough

Let's craft a precise task brief. A well-defined brief is like a detailed map for Cowork, guiding it safely and effectively to your desired destination.

1.  **Define Your Core Goal:** Start with a single, clear sentence explaining what you want Cowork to accomplish.
    *   *Example:* "Summarize the key points from the provided article."

2.  **Specify Your Inputs:** What information will Cowork receive to complete its task? Be explicit about the type and source. This ensures Cowork has all it needs and doesn't guess.
    *   *Example:* "Cowork will receive the full text of an article as a string input."

3.  **Outline the Output Format:** How should the final result look? Do you need a list, a paragraph, a specific file type? Defining this prevents irrelevant outputs.
    *   *Example:* "The output should be a bullet-point list, with each point summarizing a distinct key idea from the article. The list should contain 3-5 bullet points."

4.  **Establish Review Criteria:** How will you judge if Cowork did a good job? These criteria are your safety net, allowing you to quickly verify the quality and relevance of the output.
    *   *Example:* "The summary is accurate and captures the main ideas. Each bullet point is concise. The summary avoids introducing new information not present in the article."

By following these steps, you've created a safe, predictable task for Cowork. You've set boundaries, provided clear instructions, and defined success – a perfect foundation for your first Cowork experience!
